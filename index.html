ℹ Understanding the RevOps Decision Engine
What is it?
Determines whether business performance is driven by lead quality, advisor effectiveness, or both.

How is it calculated?
Combines Source Efficiency, Advisor Effectiveness and Advisor Quadrant analysis.

Advisor Effectiveness: Actual Qualification % ÷ Expected Qualification %. Measures advisor performance adjusted for lead quality.

Quadrants: Classifies advisors into Star Performer, Hidden Gem, Coaching Opportunity, or Lead Quality Issue.

Opportunity Index: Expected % × Lead Volume × (1 − Performance Index). Prioritizes coaching effort.

Executive Diagnosis: Determines whether the primary bottleneck is lead quality, advisor performance, or both.
